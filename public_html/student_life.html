Welcome to Strawberry University

Strawberry University is ranked #2 in the world for it's student life by ThisIsAMadeUpWebsiteCalledStudentLife.org based on its diverse range
of different activities available for all students to do 24/7. All dorms for students are former luxury apartments with more room
than most know what to do with. All meals served at Strawberry University are prepared by the world's top chefs
at no extra cost to the students. There are hundreds of clubs you can join and if none fit your needs,
it is very easy to start your own, all you need is an idea and a couple people who would like to be a part of it.

Student Life is currently looking for new ambassadors! We would love to have creative and innovative students join our team!

The ambassadors of Student Life would like your feedback on our latest activities. How do you think they went? Were the activities well run? Did you have fun? Would you participate again? If you would like to give feedback please visit the Student Life twitter page.

If you are interested in starting a group or club, have ideas for new activities, or any feedback please reach out to a Student Life Ambassador in order to get your ideas rolling! We are always looking for new and creative ideas to continue being a diverse campus!
