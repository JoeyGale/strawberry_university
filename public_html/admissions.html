Strawberry University's Admissions

Whether you are a first year, transfer, or international student, Strawberry University is excited you're thinking of joining this great school. Everything about this school is designed specifically to not only make this the best time of your life, but properly prepare you for life after college, which is one reason why we have a 100% job or graduate school placement rating within the first 3 months of graduation. We are thrilled to make this transition in your life as least difficult as possible and can't wait to see you on campus. If you have any questions or concerns, the admissions office would be excited to take your call at 555-555-5555 between the hours of 6 a.m. to 6 p.m. or you can stop by our office in the middle of campus between 6 a.m. to 5 p.m.
